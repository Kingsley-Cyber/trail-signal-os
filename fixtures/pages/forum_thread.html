Rechargeable fan died overnight — workaround?

Posted in Gear hacks · 142 replies · Last active 3 days ago

Took a 10000mAh power bank and a clip fan to Joshua Tree. Fan quit at 2am with 40% bank left. Manufacturer says "eco mode" should last 18h but that must be on low in a climate-controlled room, not a nylon tent.

Current workaround: run the fan off the bank, swap to a second bank at midnight, and keep the fan on low only. Still too loud for light sleepers in our group.

Same model. I zip-tied a small desk fan to the roof loop and wired a 12V RV adapter when car camping. Not ultralight but honest runtime.
