# Safety, Compliance and Ethics

## First-pass exclusion screen

Reject or escalate products involving:

- primary life-safety dependence;
- climbing, flotation or fall-arrest load bearing;
- combustion-system modification;
- weapons or hazardous materials;
- medical diagnosis or treatment claims;
- children’s safety without competent compliance review;
- electrical products without appropriate testing pathways;
- protected designs, marks or counterfeit positioning;
- wildlife/environmental contraband;
- invasive data collection or prohibited scraping.

## Product claims

Marketing claims must match verified testing. Avoid “waterproof,” load, temperature, safety or medical claims without an appropriate test method and records.

## Human research

For interviews, obtain consent, minimize personal data, avoid deceptive collection and preserve anonymized notes. Community posts are not permission to republish personal details.

## Environmental impact

Evaluate disposability, microplastic shedding, wildlife entanglement, battery/fuel waste, trail damage and use restrictions. A product that externalizes harm is not an elite niche.

## Escalation

This repository is a research framework, not legal or certification advice. Record jurisdiction and obtain qualified review when a candidate reaches experiment or manufacturing stage.
