# Dossier Writer Prompt

Compile a decision dossier from repository artifacts. Do not introduce new facts.

Required sections:

- one-sentence niche thesis;
- participant and job-to-be-done;
- task episode and friction;
- supporting and contradictory evidence;
- workaround and substitute map;
- product requirements and non-goals;
- current competitive and price snapshot;
- seasonality and launch timing;
- operations, returns, safety, legal/IP and policy risks;
- gate results and score breakdown;
- falsification experiment with thresholds;
- decision and confidence;
- evidence appendix.

Every material statement must cite evidence IDs or be labeled hypothesis/inference/unknown.
