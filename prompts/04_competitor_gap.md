# Competitor and Substitute Gap Prompt

Map the complete solution landscape for one friction.

Include:

1. direct products;
2. adjacent products repurposed by users;
3. DIY workarounds;
4. behavioral workarounds;
5. doing nothing;
6. premium and budget incumbents.

Compare task fit, closure/control interface, materials, dimensions, compatibility, variants, price, claims, recurring review failures, shipping burden, likely return causes and defensibility.

A gap exists only when repeated friction survives after considering substitutes. Do not call poor marketing a product gap unless the product itself already solves the task.
