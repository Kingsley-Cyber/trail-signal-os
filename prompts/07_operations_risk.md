# Operations and Risk Prompt

Screen the candidate before testing or sourcing.

Evaluate materials, dimensions, dimensional weight, fragile parts, defect modes, tolerances, attachment compatibility, SKU/variant count, packaging, moisture/odor, cleaning, expected returns, certifications, claims, safety dependency, regulatory exposure, platform restrictions, intellectual property, supplier concentration and quality-control tests.

Return:

- risk register with severity and mitigation;
- minimum product test plan;
- forbidden or unsupported claims;
- go/escalate/reject recommendation.

Do not provide legal or certification conclusions beyond verified source material.
