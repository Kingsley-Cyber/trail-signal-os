# Red-Team Prompt

Attempt to disprove the candidate.

Test these failure modes:

- the complaint is rare, old or copied;
- the task is not frequent enough;
- the workaround is already good enough;
- users will not pay to remove the friction;
- a cheap incumbent already solves it;
- search/social attention is misleading;
- the target community is hard or expensive to reach;
- variants, fit or compatibility drive returns;
- seasonality creates stranded inventory;
- safety, regulation, IP or platform rules create unacceptable risk;
- the proposed feature causes a new failure;
- the solution is easy to clone and lacks brand or system expansion.

Return strongest disconfirming evidence first, unresolved unknowns, and the cheapest experiment that could reject the thesis.
