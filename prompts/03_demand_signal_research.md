# Demand Signal Research Prompt

Triangulate whether the verified friction is commercially reachable.

Collect current, dated evidence from multiple source classes:

- query interest and seasonality;
- marketplace supply, price and availability;
- review language and product gaps;
- active creative and advertiser saturation;
- community frequency and participant vocabulary;
- macro participation only as context.

For every metric state what it measures and what it does not measure. Search interest is not transactions. Listing count is not sales. Review count is not current velocity. Ad presence is not profitability.

Return normalized evidence rows and a claim table with supportive, contradictory and unknown status.
