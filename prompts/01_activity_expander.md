# Activity Expander Prompt

Expand one broad activity into atomic task episodes without making market claims.

Return records containing activity, participant, task, context, environmental constraints, body/hand state, likely friction family, workaround hypothesis, product territory, season tags, risk flags and `fact_status=hypothesis`.

Coverage requirements:

- before, during and after the activity;
- movement and stationary phases;
- setup, use, transition, cleanup, repair and storage;
- wet, cold, hot, dark, windy, muddy, gloved and occupied-hand conditions;
- novice, frequent participant, child/family, animal companion and adaptive-access contexts when relevant.

Avoid category-level products. Prefer narrow interfaces and transitions.
